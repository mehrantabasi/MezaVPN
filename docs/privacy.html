Privacy Policy

MezaVPN is designed to provide an Android VPN connection without advertising or analytics SDKs. This policy describes information used by the official application.

Information stored on the device

Application preferences, connection settings, available server profiles, the selected server, recent delay-test results, and update times may be stored in private app storage. This information remains on the device unless the app or its data is removed.

Network activity

MezaVPN makes network requests required to retrieve service information, test connection quality, establish VPN tunnels, and confirm real internet access. Connectivity checks may contact widely available services operated by providers such as Cloudflare, Google, or Microsoft. These providers may process standard technical request information under their own policies.

VPN traffic passes through the selected server. Server operators and destination services may process technical information required to provide their services. Users should connect only to servers they trust.

Information not intentionally collected

The official application does not include advertising or analytics SDKs and does not intentionally collect browsing history, communication content, contacts, photos, precise location, or advertising identifiers. MezaVPN does not require an account.

Operational logs

The application and its infrastructure may produce limited technical logs needed for reliability, abuse prevention, or troubleshooting. Hosting and connectivity providers may retain standard access logs under their own policies.

Changes

This policy may be updated when application behavior or legal requirements change. Material changes will be published in the official repository.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue in the official repository without including sensitive information.
